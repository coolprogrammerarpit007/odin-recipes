Morning Smoothie

Description

This smoothie is a one-stop-shop for all your breakfast needs! If you're looking to gain muscle fast, then look no further

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • A handful of frozen blueberries
  • A cup of oats
  • A large spoonful of penut butter
  • 2 Cups Milk

Steps

  1. Pulse the oats in the blender
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and blend
  3. Enjoy your gains
